# 0016: NixCon Sponsorship Tier Integrity Policy

## Context and Subject
Following organizational gridlock regarding the NixCon 2026 sponsorship tiers—specifically concerning corporate entities requesting to pool funds and split single high-tier sponsorships (e.g., sharing physical booth space and tier branding), the Steering Committee stepped in to establish a high-level guardrail protecting the Foundation's financial sustainability and brand integrity.

**Proposed By:** cafkafk

## Motion
> "The SC establishes that official NixCon sponsorship tiers and their core perks (including physical booth space) may not be pooled, split, or shared between multiple independent corporate entities. Sponsors must be treated equally according to the established prospectus."
