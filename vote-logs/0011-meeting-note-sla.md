# Vote #11: Meeting note SLA

**Opened:** <2026-03-18> by cafkafk **Closed:** <2026-03-23> **Status:** ❌ Rejected

**Proposal**: To mitigate the turnaround time on meeting notes, no later than 5
days after meeting notes are published in PR form, they should be mergeable
regardless of lack of review. If no one reviews the notes within 5 days, they
should be considered able to be committed, and follow-up corrections will have
to happen in a separate PR. This does not mean that if a PR *does* have a
review, it can be ignored.
