# Async Decision Making Process

- The vote formally closes after 5 days. Not voting constitutes abstention.
- The vote initiator must specify the exact closing date and time in the vote announcement.
- The vote can close as soon as enough votes are cast to determine a clear outcome (accept/reject).
- Vote results are added to vote log thread, with `Rationale:`, unless material is confidential.
